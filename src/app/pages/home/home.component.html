Sections on the Dashboard
  1. My Profile section contains a brief information of the user - e-mail address, initials avatar.
  2. Add a new goal section contains a form to add a goal title, goal description and schedule a date - if necessary.
  3. At a glance section shows a statistics card of all goals both pending and completed.
  4. My goals section shows each goal's title, description, scheduled date and progress bar.
  5. Goal Information section displays information on to-do items required to complete the goal, a countdown if there's a scheduled date, percentage progress.
Steps on how to use the goal tracker
  1. Create an account.
  2. Sign in to access your Goal Tracker dashboard.
  3. Add a new goal and give it a description. Is it a future goal? Schedule a date!
  4. View summary of your goals on the At a glance section.
  5. Add the to-do items it'll take to complete the goal.
